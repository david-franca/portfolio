## About
Hello! I'm David França, a technology enthusiast. I'm a front-end developer, 30 years old, passionate about creating amazing digital experiences. I currently live in Fortaleza, Ceará, with my wonderful wife, and our lives are full of adventure and learning.<br /> My journey into the world of programming started about three years ago, when a supervisor challenged me to create a mobile application. At the time I knew very little about web development and basically nothing about mobile development. However, I accepted the challenge and offered to study to complete the assignment. Because of this challenge, I was fascinated by how code can bring design to life and make the web a more interactive and engaging place. Immersed in the world of front-end development over the years, I mastered HTML, CSS and languages like JavaScript to build intuitive and visually appealing interfaces.<br /> My background includes higher education in systems analysis and development and training at Rockseat , where I gained a solid foundation in web design and development concepts. Since then, I've had the opportunity to work on exciting projects, from small business websites to complex web applications.<br /> Besides coding, I'm also passionate about learning. I'm always looking for new challenges and ways to expand my skills. Outside the programming world, I am an avid reader and enjoy exploring new places with my wife. In addition, I am a religious person and regularly attend church, where I also have the opportunity to serve the community. I believe my faith guides me both at work and in daily interactions.<br /> In the future, my goal is to continue to improve my technical skills and explore mobile application development to provide a richer user experience. I am a firm believer that technology can positively change the way we interact with the world, and I look forward to playing my part in that journey.
